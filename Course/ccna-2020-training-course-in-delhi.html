What is CCNA ? Why you should do it from Uncodemy?

What will you gain?

CCNA stands for Cisco Certified Network Associate. It's a certification program offered by Cisco, a leading provider of networking equipment and technology. The CCNA certification is designed to validate a candidate's skills and knowledge in installing, configuring, and troubleshooting medium-sized routed and switched networks.

The CCNA certification is widely recognized in the IT industry and can help individuals demonstrate their expertise and advance their careers in networking. The certification covers a range of topics, including network fundamentals, network security, and network device management.

To obtain the CCNA certification, candidates must pass a written exam that covers the certification's objectives. The exam tests a candidate's theoretical knowledge as well as their practical skills in configuring and troubleshooting networks.

Overall, the CCNA certification is a valuable credential for individuals looking to build a career in networking and IT, and can help them demonstrate their skills and expertise to potential employers.

    Trends in CCNA

    Here are a few trends to keep an eye on in the world of CCNA: ...
    • Increased focus on security: As networks become more complex and interconnected, the importance of security is only increasing. CCNA professionals will need to have a strong understanding of network security principles and best practices.
    • Growing importance of cloud computing: With more and more businesses moving to the cloud, CCNA professionals will need to have a solid understanding of how to design, implement, and manage cloud-based networks.
    • Emergence of new technologies: New technologies such as 5G, IoT, and artificial intelligence are transforming the networking landscape. CCNA professionals will need to stay up-to-date with these technologies and understand how they can be integrated into network designs.
    • Greater emphasis on automation: Network automation is becoming increasingly important, as it can help reduce costs, increase efficiency, and improve network reliability. CCNA professionals will need to understand how to implement and use network automation tools.
    • Growing demand for skilled professionals: The demand for skilled CCNA professionals is expected to continue to grow, as businesses increasingly rely on networks to support their operations.
    • Increased importance of software-defined networking (SDN): SDN is a new approach to networking that allows networks to be programmed and managed dynamically, and is becoming increasingly important in the industry.
    • Growth of virtualization: Virtualization technologies are being increasingly used in networking, allowing multiple virtual networks to be created on top of a single physical network.
    • Rise of network programmability: Network programmability is the ability to automate and manage networks using code, and is becoming increasingly important in the industry.
    • Increased focus on network analytics: Network analytics is the use of data and analytics to optimize network performance, and is becoming increasingly important as networks become more complex.
    • Increased emphasis on network resilience: Network resilience is the ability of a network to withstand and recover from disruptions, and is becoming increasingly important as networks become more critical to business operations.
    • Greater focus on green networking: Green networking is the design and implementation of networks that are energy-efficient and environmentally sustainable, and is becoming increasingly important as businesses seek to reduce their environmental impact.
